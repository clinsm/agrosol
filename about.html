Bridging the Knowledge Gap

About Agrosol

Agrosol (Pty) Ltd is an agricultural consulting and management company that offers technical support and backup to the agricultural and agribusiness sectors in Africa. We provide technically, environmentally and socially compatible consulting services to developing farmers. There is a growing need to improve self- sustainability, increase productivity and maximize on profit with the limited resources that farmers have at their disposal. There is even a greater need for skills transfer and create employment in our farming communities. As a company, we realized this knowledge gap, and it is our goal to empower as many small to medium scale farmers as possible. We incubate emerging farmers into fully- fledged commercial farmers that are fit to handle sustainable enterprises, attaining improved quality yields and participating in commercial supply chains.

Our Mission

To help the agricultural and agribusiness sectors to grow and maintain a highly successful and profitable farming enterprise. This is achieved through science-based decisions, integrated agronomic support services that combines technology and latest research developments to ensure current farming demands are met with knowledge and confidence.

Our Vision

Our strategic vision is to become a leading and preferred supplier of professional services to the agricultural and agribusiness sectors through empowering farmers with adequate knowledge and skills.
