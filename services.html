What we do

A. Farm Management

1. Crop production - Successful farming is made up of many categories and different people specialize in their own area of expertise. We pride ourselves in crop production under the following systems:

• Hydroponic production
• Organic production
• Drip production
• Pivot production
• Sprinkler production

No matter you are under an intensive or extensive production system, tunnel, greenhouse or shed net system, we can help you realise your dream.

2. Rain fed crops - Your farm might not be suitable for irrigation because of several reasons which include topography. Give us the job of deciding which crops you can grow with the rains. We will be able to give you advice on the range of crops that do well in your area.


3. Organic farming - More emphasise is now being placed on sustainable ways of crop production. These include organic farming. We will make sure you realise your dream in this respect. Our experienced staff will bring the best out of your farm. 

This could be vegetables or any crop that we recommend. We will take you through the process from budgeting to production and marketing

B. Feasibility Study and Project Appraisals

Our farm management will not be anything if we cannot take somebody who is investing into farming through the crucial initial stages in the business. Let us do your feasibility studies and project appraisals. Our approach is practical, and we'll be able to put into practice what we put into the document unlike many who will do it in the office and do not have a clue how to implement it.

Do not forget that we work with our partners in all this so that you have the best

C. Human Resources

Our biggest strength is finding, placing and managing the right people for your farm. It is very difficult to get the right person if you are not well versed about the farming industry. Even the experienced farmers will struggle to get the people. Our advantage is that we have managed to keep track of all the farm managers we have worked  ith over the years. These managers have also met other managers in their line of duty. Therefore, we will be able to head-hunt the right person for your farm, small or big. It will be our duty to make sure that the manager performs to standard.

We are giving you the best person who will not let you down. We carry the responsibility of making sure that the farm is run efficiently and are accountable to the person we place. We do this confidently as we know all the people we deal with. We will place someone qualified for the job.

D. Tunnel Installations

We are in partnership with the leaders in tunnel construction. If you wish to have production under tunnels, we will help you get the best quotation and best quality. This will include the irrigation in the tunnels. If you are not sure whether you want tunnels or some other structure, talk to us. We will look at your requirements and give you recommendations.

E. Irrigation Design And Installations

We also work with our partners in this regard and will make sure that you get value for money. We run the project and handle a quality installation. We are unlike other companies that would just install and go. To us, it will be the beginning of a long relationship even if you do not give us the job to run your project. There are many areas we will do business together.

F. Marketing

Over the years we have learnt that you cannot just go into production without looking at the market to which you intend to sell your produce. Many farmers find it difficult to sell what they would have produced and at times this drives them out of business. I do not blame them for not finding a market. This is a different discipline altogether. To give a full package to our clients, we have partnered some markets that will be ready to take the produce.

G. Global Gap Pre-Audits & Preparatory

All our workshops can be adapted as private training for your organization's staff. You can also work with our highly qualified team of capacity building experts to develop customized training solutions for your company's specific needs.

H. Trainings

We have managed to partner with renowned people in this aspect. We can train from you the investor in agriculture to the last man who is weeding. Our job is to mentor everyone on the farm so that when we leave you will be able to continue and succeed. There are many other training courses that we offer that are relevant to the farming business.
